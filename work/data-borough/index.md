**Data Borough** is a competitive AI and data science platform where practitioners learn, compete, and prove their skills by solving real-world problems sourced from enterprises.

You can explore challenges for free, and upgrade to participate in enterprise-backed datathons, earn recognition, and win prizes — with clear evaluation metrics and transparent scoring.

## Why Data Borough Exists

Most “AI portfolios” are hard to trust. Data Borough creates a cleaner signal:

- **Real problems, not toy projects**  
  Challenges reflect practical constraints and measurable outcomes.

- **Transparent scoring & leaderboards**  
  Clear metrics, repeatable evaluation, and visible rankings.

- **Credibility you can carry**  
  Certificates, outcomes, and reputation that hiring managers and teams can understand.

- **Structured reviews**  
  Judge-led assessments and feedback loops for top submissions.

- **Enterprise-grade challenge environments**  
  Designed to keep sensitive contexts controlled while still enabling meaningful participation.

## Who It’s For

- Data Scientists and ML Engineers who want sharper proof of skill  
- Analysts moving into applied AI  
- Builders who learn best by shipping under constraints

If you want to **build**, **learn**, and **get noticed**, Data Borough is your arena.
