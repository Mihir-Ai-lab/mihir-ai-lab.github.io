**VaultNest** is an enterprise-grade AI Studio and trust enforcement layer for building, deploying, and governing AI in regulated environments.

It enables compliant collaboration between regulated data entities—such as banks, healthcare organisations, and large consumer platforms—and third-party AI suppliers, without compromising data sovereignty, model IP, or auditability.

VaultNest allows organisations to work with **whoever they need to**, **wherever they are**, and **on whatever protected data is required**—within clearly defined governance boundaries.

---

## What VaultNest Enables

AI adoption in regulated sectors often fails because governance and execution are treated as separate problems. VaultNest unifies them.

### 1. Governed AI collaboration  
Teams can collaborate across organisations while enforcing strict controls over how data and models are accessed, used, and executed.

### 2. Independent control for data and model owners  
A **dual-key control architecture** ensures that:
- Data owners retain authority over data usage
- Model owners retain protection over intellectual property  
No single party can unilaterally bypass controls.

### 3. Auditability by default  
Every action—from dataset access to model execution—is logged, traceable, and explainable, supporting internal audits and regulatory reviews.

---

## Core Capabilities

- Secure AI Studio for working with regulated data  
- Dual-key access control for data and model IP protection  
- Tamper-resistant audit logs, consent records, and execution trails  
- Escrow-based custody for sensitive AI artifacts  
- Designed for DPDP, GDPR, PDPL, and enterprise compliance  
- Supports on-prem, hybrid, and containerised environments  

VaultNest is built for **real operational AI**, not isolated pilots or proofs of concept.
